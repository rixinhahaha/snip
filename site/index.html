Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for power users who take screenshots every day. Fast capture, rich annotation, AI-powered organization.

Instant Capture

Hit Cmd+Shift+2 anywhere. Select a region or grab the full screen. Your snip is on your clipboard in under 5 seconds.

Powerful Annotation

Rectangles, arrows, text, tags, blur brush, and AI-powered object segmentation. Everything you need to communicate visually.

AI Organization

A local vision AI names, categorizes, and tags every saved screenshot automatically. No cloud. No API keys. Runs entirely on your Mac.

Semantic Search

Find any screenshot by describing what was in it. "slack message about deployment" finds the right one. Local embeddings, no API calls.

Native macOS Feel

Menu bar app with Liquid Glass UI. No Dock icon. Works across Spaces. Stays out of your way until you need it.

2GIF Animation

Segment any object from your screenshot, then animate it into a GIF with AI-generated motion presets. Bring your screenshots to life.

How It Works

Four steps. Five seconds.

From capture to clipboard in a flash. Save to disk and AI handles the rest.

1

Capture

Cmd+Shift+2 opens a fullscreen overlay. Drag to select any region, or press Enter for full screen.

2

Annotate

Add rectangles, arrows, text, blur, or use AI segmentation to isolate objects. All with keyboard shortcuts.

3

Copy or Save

Press Esc to copy to clipboard. Press Cmd+S to save to disk and trigger AI organization.

4

Find Later

AI names, categorizes, and indexes your screenshots. Search by description with Cmd+Shift+F.

Auto-Tagging

AI generates relevant tags for every screenshot — "IRS", "tax form", "social media", "code architecture" — making them instantly discoverable through the tag cloud.

Auto-Categorize

Screenshots are sorted into folders: code, chat, web, design, documents, terminal, and more. Create custom categories too.

Smart Naming

No more "Screenshot 2024-01-15 at 3.45.23 PM". Every snip gets a descriptive name like "api-endpoint-response-fix".

Semantic Search

384-dimensional embeddings make every screenshot searchable by meaning, not just filename. Powered by Transformers.js, all local.

100% Private

All AI processing happens on your Mac using Ollama and Metal GPU acceleration. Zero data sent to the cloud. No API keys needed for core features.
